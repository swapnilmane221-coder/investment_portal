Frequently Asked Questions about Stock

1. What is a stock?
A stock represents ownership in a company. When you buy a stock, you own a portion of the company, called shares.
2. How do I buy stocks?
You can buy stocks through a brokerage account, either online or with the help of a broker. Some apps also allow stock trading.
3. What is the stock market?
The stock market is a platform where stocks are bought and sold. Major stock markets include the NYSE and NASDAQ.
4. What affects stock prices?
Stock prices are affected by factors like company performance, market trends, economic conditions, and investor sentiment.
5. What are dividends?
Dividends are payments made by a company to its shareholders, usually from profits. Not all companies pay dividends.
